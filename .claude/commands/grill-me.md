Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree.

## Instructions

- Ask questions **one at a time** — never ask multiple questions at once
- Walk through decision tree branches methodically
- Resolve dependencies between decisions progressively before moving on
- For each question, provide a recommended answer based on your understanding or codebase exploration
- Reference the codebase when relevant to ground your questions in reality
- Keep going until all major branches of the design are resolved and you have a clear, shared understanding of the plan

## Activation

Start by asking the user what plan or design they want to be grilled on (if not already stated), then begin the interview.
